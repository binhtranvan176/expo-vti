How to install Expo old version on Iphone

  1. Step 1

    Open this URL by Safari

    Click button "Install" to start

    Wait for the popup to show then choose "Install"

  2. Step 2

    Go to "Setting" choose "General"

    Then find and choose "VPN & Device Management"

    Then select the enterprise app

  3. Step 3

    Choose "Trust ..."

    Wait for the popup to appear then click "Trust"

  4. Step 4

    If Expo require "Develop mode" please turn it on

    Open "Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > "Developer Mode". Enable the toggle
